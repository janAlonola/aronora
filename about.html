About Me

I like to call myself Simon Bebek formally, or Argon, Aronora, jan Alonola and so on.

I'm from Germany with ancestry from Silesia. I speak Silesian, but only through my parents, so my vocabulary is limited.

In my freetime I do a lot of things, as playing violin for example. Or making YouTube videos on my channel.

I co-founded lipu tenpo e.V. and am current member of the board.

Despite my natlangs German, Silesian, English, I also speak Toki Pona fairly fluently and am interested in Globasa and Kokanu.
